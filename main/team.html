Group members

We are a diverse and collaborative team of faculty, postdoctoral researchers, PhD students and undergraduates. Our work spans quantum error correction, fault-tolerance, error mitigation, quantum cryptography, open quantum systems, and other core problems in quantum information theory. By combining rigorous mathematical analysis with physically motivated models, we aim to develop scalable, secure, and fault-tolerant frameworks for cutting-edge future quantum technologies.
