Active research directions

Our work spans foundational questions in quantum theory and practical architectures for near-term and fault-tolerant quantum technologies.

Quantum Error Correction & Fault Tolerance

Design of noise-adapted codes, decoding algorithms, and fault-tolerant protocols tailored to realistic hardware noise models.

Surface & LDPC codes Subsystem codes Noise tailoring

Topological & Fracton Phases

Study of exotic phases of matter with constrained dynamics, emergent gauge structures, and robust quantum memory.

X-cube model Gauge theories Quantum memories

Quantum Optics & Photonic Platforms

Quantum light-matter interfaces for communication, sensing, and simulation using integrated and cavity photonics.

Nonclassical light Quantum networks Integrated photonics
