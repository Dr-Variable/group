How to apply

A short guide you can tailor to your own application process.

1. Prepare your material

Typically this includes a CV, a concise research statement (1–2 pages), and the names of 2–3 referees.

2. Send your application

Email your material as a single PDF to group-applications@example.edu with a clear subject line.

3. Follow-up & interviews

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an online interview and may be asked to give a short research presentation.
